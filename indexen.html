About Us

Hassan Quran Center is dedicated to providing high-quality Quran education to students worldwide through interactive online classes on Zoom. Our experienced and qualified teachers offer personalized lessons for all age groups, focusing on proper recitation (Tajweed), memorization (Hifz), and understanding of the Quran. We aim to make Quran learning accessible and engaging, allowing students to study from the comfort of their homes. Our flexible class schedules, one-on-one and group sessions, and structured curriculum ensure that each student progresses at their own pace. Join Hassan Quran Center today and start your journey of learning the Holy Quran with expert guidance in a convenient and effective way!.
